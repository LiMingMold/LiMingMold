甚麼是CSR

Definition

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), means company should not only creates benefits for stakeholders, but also protects the environment and gives back to the society. Enterprise should be responsible citizens too. Stakeholders are all people related to the operation of the company, including employees, customers, suppliers, consumers, communities, countries, and the natural environment.





我們的願景

Our vision & plans

In the face of changes in the global climate, market and environment, Liming, as a member of the global village, we firmly believe that in the pursuit of benefit and performance, enterprises should also fulfill their corporate social responsibilities.We roughly divide corporate social responsibility into four parts and discuss them; they are:


Employee cares:

Build a friendly working environment, and establish an appropriate promotion policy and welfares; we also care about our employees’ health too.

Customer services:

Partnership maintenance and strengthen; improve our customer communication channels.

Social contributions:

Support developments of local communities, give back to social charity organizations, and participate in charitable activities.

Environmental protections:

Energies conservation strategies and guidelines, wastes disposal guidelines.
