WHAT WE MAKE?

  • Products with high cleanliness require
    We have a HEPA positive pressure high cleanliness manufacturing workshop, which is about 1000 m² big, is very suitable for produce medical or biomedical products.
  • Products with total solution
    We integrated the injection mold manufacture, injection molding, surface coating, assemble, quality test and the package. We provide the complete one-stop service.
  • Products with insert/over molding require
    We have many experiences in insert molding and over molding. Besides, the newly created automation department is timely matched to reduce manpower, cost, and improving yield.
  • Products with two shot injection molding
    We are equipped with double materials injection machine, which can produce two shot products with soft and hard materials. Basically, two shot injection molding will have better bonding strength than insert molding.

Not only that, we can produce almost any thermoplastic injection products, from early analysis to post-process processing, from soft rubber to hard plastic, we do everything.
